OCaml 2013

The OCaml Users and Developers Workshop

Boston (MA, USA), September 24, 2013

Colocated with ICFP 2013

Participate

The meeting is an informal community gathering of users of the language, library authors, and developers, using and extending OCaml in new ways.

History

The first occurrence of the OCaml Users and Developers Workshop was colocated with ICFP 2012, in Copenhagen, following the OCaml Meetings in Paris in 2010 and 2011. Building on the success of past events, OCaml 2013 will be held on September 24, 2013, in Boston, colocated with ICFP 2013.
